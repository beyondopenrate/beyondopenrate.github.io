Our Craft

Check out our full swag of services geared to turbocharge every bit of your customer yarn. Peek into how our specialised solutions can shake up your email marketing strategy and hook you up with your customers like never before.

Email Strategy Development

We analyse your current marketing tactics and business goals to whip up a bespoke email marketing strategy that cranks up engagement and conversions. Our strategic planning includes segmentation, personalised yarns, and timing to ensure ace results.

Campaign Design & Execution

Our crew whips up eye-catching and gutsy email campaigns that echo your brand's vibe and hit the mark with your target crowd. From the initial spark to full throttle, we handle every bit, including A/B testing and tweaking based on performance data.

List Management & Segmentation

Top-notch list management is the backbone of ace email marketing. We help you build, maintain, and divvy up your email lists to target the right mob with the right yarns, boosting relevance and scoring better results.

Automation & Workflow Integration

Automate your email communications with our advanced workflow solutions that trigger based on user behavior and preferences. From welcome sequences to re-engagement campaigns, we ensure that your emails reach your customers at the ideal moment.

Performance Analytics & Reporting

With our comprehensive analytics services, we track the performance of your campaigns and provide detailed reports. Insights cover various metrics such as open rates, click-through rates, and conversions, empowering you with the knowledge to make informed decisions.

Compliance & Deliverability Optimisation

We ensure that your email campaigns comply with all relevant regulations, including GDPR and CAN-SPAM, to maintain your brand's reputation and trustworthiness. Additionally, we optimise deliverability to ensure your messages reach your audience's inbox, not their spam folder.
