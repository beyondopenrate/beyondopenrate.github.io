Email Marketing & Automation

Email marketing is the top dog for bang-for-your-buck across all marketing channels, both old school and digital.

It's like having a chinwag in someone's inbox—just you and them. When you nail it, it’s the best tool in your kit for keeping tabs on leads and keeping the cash register ringing with repeat sales.

Forget spamming your mates with just newsletters and company yarns. These days, it's all about crafting spot-on messages for the right bunch of your list at the perfect time they’re likely to take a squiz.

This course is a deep dive into the guts of managing your email list, hooking in leads with top-notch magnets, and tracking the right numbers to really kick your organisation’s email marketing up a notch.

You’ll get the lowdown on the two main brews of Email Marketing:

Email Campaigns: Firing off a message to your list, or just a part of it, all in one go.
Email Automations: A series of set-and-forget emails that roll out over time, kicking in when someone grabs a freebie from your site.

From strategising to A/B testing and everything in between, this course will show you how email can sharpen your industry cred, pull in more customers, and turn them into true blue brand fans.

Who’s this for?

Spot on for any individual, social media freelancer/agency, or small to medium business owner keen to get the skills and savvy to whip up and whip out email marketing campaigns that build solid customer relationships and boost business growth. Ready to become a legend in email marketing? This is your ticket.

Learning Outcomes

By the time you've smashed through this course, you'll be able to:

  • Get your head around what email marketing is all about, why it’s a ripper for your organisation, and how chucking it into your marketing mix can be a game-changer.
  • Learn the different types of emails you can shoot out, and get the lowdown on the top tools for setting up, designing, testing, and launching your campaigns.
  • Wrap your mind around the ins and outs of email automation and how to sync them up with your email campaigns for maximum impact.
  • Craft a cracker of an email marketing strategy, including how to segment your audience, set clear goals, nail the timing, and cook up engaging content.
  • Turbocharge your email list using lead magnets on your landing pages and website that are too good to resist.
  • Learn how to dodge the dreaded spam and promo folders, so your emails land right where you want ‘em.
  • Design emails that get clicks, from crafting killer subject lines and personalising messages to picking the right trigger words and calls to action.
  • Get savvy with A/B testing, split testing, and deliverability checks to boost your performance, and figure out what bits to test for the best results.
  • Understand the key metrics to watch and how to tweak your approach to pump up your results.
